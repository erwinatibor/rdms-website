Who We Are

Rwanda's Dento-Medical Society

A nonprofit professional organization uniting dental and medical students and professionals to advance oral and public health across Rwanda and beyond.

🦷
Our Vision

Advancing Oral & Public Health Through Innovation and Partnership

RDMS Rwanda is a nonprofit, professional organization based in Ngoma, Huye, in the south of Rwanda. It unites dental and medical students and professionals to promote oral and public health through outreach, research, innovation, and partnerships that advance healthcare in Rwanda and beyond.

Our Story

In Rwanda, oral health has long existed at the margins of the national healthcare system — underfunded, underrepresented, and often invisible to the communities that need it most. Dental disease, though largely preventable, continues to affect children, adults, and vulnerable populations who lack access to even the most basic oral care.

RDMS was founded in 2024 to confront this reality head-on. Driven by a shared belief that oral health is inseparable from general health, our founders built a society that bridges the gap between dentistry and medicine — bringing professionals, students, and communities together under one mission.

Registered under RDB as a Non-Profit Limited by Guarantee (Code: 143885158), RDMS operates from Huye and reaches communities across Rwanda through field programs, research, education, and policy engagement.

Our Mission

"To unite dental surgeons, dental therapists, and public health professionals in advancing oral-systemic health through collaborative education, research, outreach, and policy advocacy."

📍 Where We Operate

Headquartered in Ngoma, Huye (Southern Province), RDMS conducts programs across Rwanda — from urban clinics and university campuses to rural schools and underserved communities.

🤝 Who We Bring Together

Our members include dental surgeons, dental therapists, medical doctors, public health professionals, and students from across Rwanda's health sciences community — all united by one purpose.

🌍 Our Reach

While rooted in Rwanda, RDMS engages with global oral health networks, international awareness days, and cross-border partnerships — contributing to healthcare advancement beyond Rwanda's borders.

What We Do

Our Core Services

From school screenings and preventive programs to research and professional education — RDMS delivers integrated oral health services across Rwanda.

🏫

School-Based Dental Screenings

Healthy teeth start young. RDMS conducts systematic dental screenings at primary and secondary schools across Rwanda, identifying untreated caries, gum problems, and developmental issues before they become serious. Our teams visit schools with portable equipment, assess students, and connect those who need follow-up care with local dental services. We also provide on-the-spot oral hygiene education to students and teachers alike — making schools a frontline of oral health prevention.

🦷

Preventive Care Programs

Prevention is more powerful — and more affordable — than treatment. RDMS runs structured preventive care programs that reach communities through multiple channels: daily oral health messaging via SMS and social media, fluoride application sessions, oral hygiene kits distribution, and community workshops on brushing, diet, and dental habits. Our preventive programs are designed for schools, health centers, and community groups, with content tailored to both children and adults. The goal is simple — reduce dental disease before it starts.

View care programs →
📊

Oral Health Research & Data Collection

Evidence drives change. RDMS conducts and supports oral health research across Rwanda — collecting data on disease prevalence, care access, and health behaviors to build the evidence base needed for policy reform. We collaborate with academic institutions, hospitals, and international research bodies to generate studies that are both locally relevant and globally significant. Our research ranges from cross-sectional community surveys to clinical outcome studies, all contributing to a richer understanding of Rwanda's oral health landscape.

Explore our research →
📰

Dental Medicine Chronicles

Knowledge should be shared. The Dental Medicine Chronicles is RDMS's regular publication series — covering clinical case studies, public health updates, policy commentary, and innovations in dental medicine. Written by and for dental and medical professionals in Rwanda, the Chronicles bridge the gap between academic research and everyday clinical practice. Whether you are a dental student, a practicing surgeon, or a health policy professional, the Chronicles offer insights that are practical, timely, and Rwanda-focused.

Read the Chronicles →
🎓

RDMS Research Academy

Continuous learning is the backbone of a strong health system. The RDMS Research Academy is a structured educational platform providing dental and health professionals in Rwanda with access to curated research lessons, case-based discussions, and continuing professional development content. Topics range from oral-systemic health links and preventive dentistry to emerging clinical techniques and public health policy. Lessons are designed to be accessible and practically applicable — equipping professionals to deliver better, more informed care to their patients and communities.

Join the Academy →
🚐

Community Mobile Clinics

Access to dental care should not depend on where you live. RDMS deploys mobile dental clinics into underserved rural and peri-urban communities across Rwanda — bringing professional oral healthcare directly to people who might otherwise never see a dentist. Services include free dental examinations, extractions, fluoride treatment, and distribution of oral hygiene supplies. Mobile clinic missions are planned in coordination with local health authorities and community leaders, ensuring that our visits are welcomed, well-organized, and impactful.
